Discover Zimmer Rest Unawatuna, a delightful 13-room guesthouse where comfort meets the charm of Sri Lanka’s south coast. Located on Yaddehimulla Road, just a 2-minute walk from the iconic Unawatuna Beach, our family-run haven is rated 9.2/10 for its unbeatable location by 101 real guests. Whether you’re seeking relaxation or adventure, Zimmer Rest offers a home-like escape for families, couples, and solo travelers alike.
